Welcome to the GitHub Io mirror site of newtFire.org, the development site for Elisa Beshero-Bondar’s digital humanities training materials and projects. newtFire is hosted by the Pittsburgh Supercomputing Center as part of its Data Exacell program. These pages are here as an alternative resource should the newtfire.org site be inaccessible.
