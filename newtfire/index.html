Welcome to newtFire.org, the development site for Elisa Beshero-Bondar’s digital humanities training materials and projects. newtFire is hosted by the Pittsburgh Supercomputing Center as part of its Data Exacell program.

Course Materials

  • Digital Humanities Courses: Either of these courses satisfies a core requirement in Pitt-Greensburg’s Digital Studies Certificate program.
    • Coding and Digital Archives
    • Coding and Data Visualization
  • Nineteenth-Century British Literature: This course features online annotation projects, and fulfills a research (or R elective in Pitt-Greensburg’s Digital Studies Certificate program.
  • Fantasy and Romance: This course features an exploration of game environments on virtual worlds in relation to those constructed in fantasy literature. Also fulfills a research (or R elective in Pitt-Greensburg’s Digital Studies Certificate program.
  • Digital Mitford Coding School: Training and Workshop Materials

  • Digital Amadis: A Study in Translation
