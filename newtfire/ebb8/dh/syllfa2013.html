We ask that you:
    • Be sure your coding is consistent--that dates and paragraphs are all marked the same way consistently throughout your file, for example.
    • For markup of cultural information or cultural contact events (like exchange of goods, thefts, rituals, communication, etc), be sure that you use some elements more than once, and that these are used across both entries.
    • Be sure that you're defining elements and attributes in a way useful for cataloging and research--moving from the general to the more specific, and without unduly repeating the language of the journal entry in your tag names.
